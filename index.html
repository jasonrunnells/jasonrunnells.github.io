Hi, I'm Jason Runnells.

Venice, CA

I am currently studying Geography/Environmental Studies at UCLA with a focus on Geospatial Information Systems & Technologies. I am a GIS lead for The Canyon Alliance (formally the Santa Monica Civic Association), where I create and manage geodatabases, maps, and tools that support disaster preparedness and planning in response to the 2025 Palisades fire. I'm seeking opportunities where I can contribute to meaningful spatial insights, and continue growing as a geospatial professional.
